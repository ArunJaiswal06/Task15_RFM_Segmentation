Customer Segment Business Actions

1. Champions
- Offer exclusive VIP rewards and early access to new products
- Provide premium loyalty programs and personalized recommendations
- Encourage referrals and product reviews

2. Loyal Customers
- Offer membership benefits and reward points
- Cross-sell and upsell related products
- Send personalized email campaigns with special discounts

3. Potential Loyalists
- Provide discount coupons to encourage repeat purchases
- Send targeted promotional emails
- Offer bundle deals to increase purchase value

4. At Risk Customers
- Send re-engagement emails with limited-time offers
- Offer special comeback discounts
- Collect feedback to understand customer drop-off reasons

5. Lost Customers
- Launch win-back campaigns with heavy discounts
- Send reminder emails highlighting new products
- Run targeted ads to re-attract them
